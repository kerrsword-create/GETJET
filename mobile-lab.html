Mobile Laboratory

On-site analytical capability. HAPSITE ER GC/MS onboard. Same-day turnaround for adaptive investigations.

Capability

Data where decisions happen — on site.

JET operates a fully-equipped mobile laboratory that deploys to the project and delivers real-time analytical data — collapsing investigation cycles from weeks to hours.

Onboard instrumentation includes the INFICON HAPSITE ER portable GC/MS, plus field-portable screening equipment (Scorpion AX-1 PID) for pre-run characterization.

Best fit: adaptive-scope Phase II investigations, dynamic remediation monitoring, vapor intrusion surveys, and emergency response where field decisions drive the next sample location.
