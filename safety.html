Programs & Procedures

How we stay injury-free.

Orientation & Onboarding

Every new hire completes a formal Health and Safety Orientation before their first site deployment.

Daily Discipline

Mandatory daily tailgate safety meetings. Monthly all-hands safety meetings. Special Safety Committee for program improvement.

Site-Specific Planning

Task-Specific Job Safety Analysis and Site-Specific Health and Safety Plans on every mobilization.

Hazard Control

PPE Program, Lock-Out / Tag-Out Program, Hazard Communication, and active Near-Miss reporting policy.

Medical & Substance

Formal Medical Surveillance Program and Substance Abuse Program cover every JET employee.

Client Certifications

JET is certified for work under Exxon/Mobil, Shell, DOD, DOE, DOT, and Intel security/safety programs.
