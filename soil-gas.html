Soil Gas Sampling

Active and passive collection into Summa canisters, Tedlar bags, syringes, or GORE-Sorber modules — per JET SOP, compliant with all EPA and ADEQ guidelines.

Method Detail

Active & passive vapor collection.

Soil gas samples are collected in Summa canisters, Tedlar bags, syringes, or GORE-Sorber modules, with the collection media matched to analyte list, concentration range, and study duration.

Ask for the JET SOP, which complies with all EPA and ADEQ guidelines for soil gas collection and analysis.

Typical applications: landfill gas surveys, commercial property Phase II assessments, Sick Building Syndrome investigations, drycleaners, and manufacturing facilities.
