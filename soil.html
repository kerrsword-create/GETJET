Soil Sampling

Genuine Geoprobe samplers. 24–60″ clear PVC or CAB liners. Regulatory-friendly with minimal IDW and no VOC loss.

Method Detail

Defensible sampling — start to finish.

We utilize Genuine Geoprobe samplers and liners. 24–60 inch clear PVC or CAB liners allow for visual identification of stained soils and easy classification or logging with no VOC loss or sample disturbance.

The liners can be cut to any length allowing multiple discrete samples per sleeve. Regulatory-friendly methodology with minimal IDW generated — no cuttings.

Why this matters: Visual inspection of intact cores lets field geologists log stratigraphy and flag stained intervals before analytical results return — shortening decision cycles on adaptive scopes.
