About JET

An Arizona corporation. A flawless safety record since 1997.

Johnson Environmental Technologies, Inc. is an Arizona Corporation headquartered in Fountain Hills, offering a diverse range of field support services to the environmental industry: soil, soil gas, and groundwater sampling; underground utility locating; environmental excavation; indoor air quality / vapor intrusion surveys; and design/build remediation systems for contaminated soils, groundwater, and air.

Founded in 1997, incorporated 1999, and led by President R. Scott Johnson — whose environmental career began in 1992 building out mobile laboratory, soil vapor, and direct-push sampling programs.
