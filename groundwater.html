Groundwater Sampling

Grab samples, permanent monitoring wells, and free-product identification — using slotted PVC, Teflon bailers, and Geoprobe pre-pack well products.

Method Detail

Sampling and monitoring well installation.

High-quality grab samples are achieved using slotted PVC and bailers. Teflon bailers allow for quick visual recognition of free product.

Quick samples are obtained through rods via tubing with check valves — ideal for reconnaissance and short-scope investigations.

Permanent monitoring wells are installed using conventional techniques (well sand, bentonite, etc.) or through Geoprobe brand pre-pack well products for accelerated completion on DPT-accessible sites.

Free-product identification: Teflon bailers make LNAPL and DNAPL presence immediately visible in the field — informing the rest of the sampling plan in real time.
